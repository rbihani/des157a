Rules

One player steps up to the table to face the dealer. When it's your turn, throw the dice and watch those numbers light up. The total of your roll gets added to your score ... unless Lady Luck turns her back and one of those dice comes up as a "one". If that happens - BAM - your turn is over, and the dice goes back to the dealer.

But wait! After every roll, you face a choice: keep rolling and rack up those points or play it safe and pass back to the dealer before your luck runs dry. Remember, the first person to rack up 30 points or more wins the jackpot!
